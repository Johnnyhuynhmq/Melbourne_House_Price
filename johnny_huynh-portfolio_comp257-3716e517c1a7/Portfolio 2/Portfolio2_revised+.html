Experiment with different types of Regression, such as Ridge and Lasso. They are different from Linear Regression at the objective functions (adding magnitude of the coefficients) Linear Regression: Objective = RSS (minimized residual sum of squares)

Ridge Regression: Objective = RSS + alpha x sum of squares of coefficients

Lasso Regression: Objective = RSS + alpha x sum of absolute values of coefficients
